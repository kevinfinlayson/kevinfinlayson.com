About

Hi, I’m Kevin Finlayson. I’m a Montréal-based designer. I specialize in visual and interaction design, mostly for web and screen-based media. I’m also a capable front-end developer, with some full-stack experience.

From 2006-2012 I freelanced under the name Dust and Mold Design. My clients included Mozilla, Warner Music Canada, MetaLab Design, Paper Bag Records, Last Gang Entertainment and many others.

Since 2012 I’ve been a designer at Pixel Union, MetaLab Design’s digital product team. My theme designs for Shopify and Tumblr have been licensed by companies like Tesla Motors, N+1, Rdio, the Today Show, the LA Lakers and The Manual.

My background is in fine arts, specifically drawing and print media. I have an MFA from Concordia University (Montréal, 2005), and a BFA from York University (Toronto, 2002), where I also studied film, literature, critical theory, and Japanese. I used to play drums in jazz and hardcore bands, recording and touring throughout the US and Europe.

Now I’m mostly into design, front-end development, typography, running, cooking, and baseball.

Follow me on Twitter, or check out some outdated works-in-progress over on Dribbble.
